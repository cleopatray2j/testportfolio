About me

I'm Asma, a web developer based in Washington. The curiosity that is buried deep inside me must be what inspired me to move from Alexandria, Egypt to Dubai, to join Emirates Airlines. There, working at the HR department, I traveled all over the world helping the company recruit cabin crew. Traveling developed my sense of knowledge and enhanced my experience in different cultures. While working full time, I managed to earn a bachelor degree in Law. Studying law and using logic to solve problems lead me to think about joining the coding community professionally when I decided to change careers. I studied coding at George Washington University and graduated in January 2018. I focus on writing clean, elegant and efficient code. Driven by my passion for art, especially my love for painting and playing the Piano, developed my sense of creativity and got me interested in designing beautiful and functional user experience. I enjoy projects that continuously challenge me with new skills. I strive to stay current to meet those challenges with tools well suited for the job at hand.
